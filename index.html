How it works

Simple. Transparent. Yours to control.

Whether you have GHS 10 or GHS 1,000 spare, Manbi puts your money to work in days — not years.

01
💳
Top up your vault

Add funds via MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, or AirtelTigo in seconds. Even GHS 10 gets you started.

02
🔍
Browse real people

Every entrepreneur is verified. See their story, their business, and exactly what they need the money for.

03
🌟
Fund and follow

Tap to fund. Get short video updates from entrepreneurs as their business grows.

04
🔄
Get repaid. Repeat.

Repayments land back in your vault. Reinvest and watch your impact multiply.
